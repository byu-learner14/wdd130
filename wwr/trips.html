Featured Trips

Calm River Explorer

Class I–II • Beginner / Family Friendly

Perfect introduction to rafting. Gentle waves, beautiful scenery, wildlife spotting, and plenty of time to relax and splash around. Ideal for kids 6+ and first-timers.

  • Duration: Half day (3–4 hours)
  • Price: $89 per person
  • Includes: Guide, gear, snacks

Thrill Seeker Run

Class III–IV • Intermediate / Adventurous

Big waves, exciting drops, and technical maneuvers. You'll paddle hard, laugh a lot, and feel the adrenaline. Minimum age 13, previous experience recommended but not required.

  • Duration: Full day (6–7 hours)
  • Price: $149 per person
  • Includes: Lunch, professional photos available

Extreme Conyon Challenge

Class IV–V • Advanced / Expert

Intense, continuous rapids, steep drops, narrow canyons. Demands strong paddling skills, teamwork, and respect for the river. Minimum age 16, prior Class III+ experience required.

  • Duration: Full day (8+ hours)
  • Price: $199 per person
  • Includes: Gourmet riverside lunch, safety briefing
