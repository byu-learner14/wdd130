History

Founded in 2010 by a group of lifelong river enthusiasts, White Water Rafting Company began with a single raft and a dream to share the magic of wild rivers with others. What started as weekend trips on local streams has grown into one of the most respected rafting outfits in the region, guiding thousands of guests through breathtaking canyons and roaring rapids every season.
